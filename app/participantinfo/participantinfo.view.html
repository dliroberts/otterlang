When you click 'start study', after a few seconds, you'll hear a number being read out. Please type this number into the box and hit 'enter' as quickly as possible. A few things to bear in mind:

  • Make sure your speakers are on! Click here to test that the volume is set appropriately.
  • Type digits, not words: 83, not eighty-three.
  • 25 numbers will be read out. Each one is between 0 and 100.
  • The numbers are read out in a random order.
